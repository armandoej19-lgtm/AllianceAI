expenses, and equitable relief. Other cases alleging similar causes of action

different plaintiff investor. On January 19, 2016, an order was entered in connection

the District Court also allowed plaintiffs to file amended complaints as to the
remaining, non-dismissed trusts, if they so chose, and three amended complaints
