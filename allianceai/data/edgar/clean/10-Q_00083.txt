In connection with the preparation of the Original , the Company currently is experiencing disruptions in its normal processes and interactions with its accounting personnel and others involved in the preparation of the Original . Therefore, due to COVID-19&#8217;s interference in the Company&#8217;s operations, the Company was unable to file the Original  prior to the due date. Consistent with the Company&#8217;s statements made in the Form 8-K, the Company filed its Original  on May 28, 2020 (which was within the permitted timeframe of the Order).
